About Me


My name is Jairo, and I am a web application developer and graphic designer, among other things.

I currently live in Madrid, the city where I was born. I love traveling, nature, the countryside, spending time with family and friends, and practicing sports, especially basketball, cycling, and in general, any activity that makes me feel alive

After completing bachelor of Arts, I decided to study a higher degree in editorial design and production at the I.E.S Europa in Móstoles. Unfortunately, after finishing I could not dedicate myself to it due to the employment situation in the country.

Because of this, I had to start in the job market doing different activities, as a factory laborer, storekeeper, gardener, painter, pollster, telephone salesperson, and some other little thing.

Between these comings and goings, I entered the wonderful career of social work, at the Complutense University of Madrid. They were incredible years, which I remember with great affection and which I recommend to everyone, since it helped me improve many personal and professional aspects.

After finishing my studies, I couldn't dedicate myself to it either, but I had the opportunity to work as a graphic designer in a local magazine. Here I was able to enjoy and learn about web design, something I was unaware of until then.

After a while, I started working as a home help coordinator, the place where I had done my university internship.

While I was working, I decided to return to the path of web design and development, studying a web application development technician at the I.E.S. Gaspar Melchor de Jovellanos in Fuenlabrada. Having the opportunity, once I finish my studies, to work as a web developer for Sia Group, a cybersecurity company.
